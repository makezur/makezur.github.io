Kirill Mazur

I'm a third-year PhD student in the Dyson Robotics Lab at Imperial College London, supervised by Prof. Andrew Davison. I am mostly interested in novel 3D representations and their applications to computer and robotic vision.

Previously, I spent a year working at Samsung AI Center (SAIC). I also completed my Master's degree at Skoltech under the joint supervision of Victor Lempitsky and Gonzalo Ferrer.
